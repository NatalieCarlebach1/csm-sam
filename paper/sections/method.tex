\section{Method}
\label{sec:method}

CSM-SAM keeps the SAM2 image encoder and mask decoder
frozen and introduces two trainable components totalling roughly 2M
parameters. The core module, \texttt{CrossSessionMemoryAttention}
(see \texttt{csmsam/modeling/cross\_session\_memory\_attention.py}),
encodes the pre-session image and mask into a memory bank of tokens and
lets the mid-session query tokens attend to this bank through a standard
multi-head cross-attention block, conditioned on a continuous
weeks-elapsed embedding. A lightweight change head~\cite{fang2022snunet}
supervised by the pre/mid mask XOR provides an auxiliary gradient that
aligns the attention with regions of temporal change. The total loss is
\(\mathcal{L} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{Dice}} + \mathcal{L}_{\text{CE}} +
0.3\,\mathcal{L}_{\text{change}}\).
